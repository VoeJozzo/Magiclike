Dread Wraith: A spectral wraith in a tattered black shroud hovers in mid-air above a fallen soldier in dented steel plate armor, drawing a luminous wisp of pale life essence upward from the dying soldier's mouth into its own. Twilight battlefield setting; the wraith hangs weightlessly with no contact to the ground, head bowed toward its prey, a deep cowl obscuring its skull-like face. The soldier lies sprawled on his back in blood-soaked mud, eyes half-open, the last breath visibly flowing upward as a thin glowing thread. The only source of light is the life-wisp itself and a faint glow from the wraith's hollow eyes. The background is a dim purple-and-charcoal twilight sky over muddy battlefield ground.

Visionary Sage: An elderly sage with a long white beard and weathered face, dressed in deep blue robes embroidered with silver arcane symbols, head tilted back mid-trance, eyes wide and glowing pure cerulean, lips parted in awe. Translucent ghostly fragments of visions — faces, distant landscapes, fractured runes — swirl in a luminous halo around his head, orbiting his temples. His hands are raised slightly, fingers half-curled as if catching the visions. The only source of light is the glow from his eyes and the swirling vision-wisps themselves. The background is a dim shadowed stone chamber, faintly visible bookshelves and arcane diagrams on the walls dissolving into darkness.

Dawn Angel: An angel with broad ivory-feathered wings outstretched mid-descent, dressed in flowing white robes trimmed with polished gold and silver plate at the shoulders, descending diagonally from the upper-left of the frame toward a wounded soldier kneeling in the lower-right. Both hands are extended toward the soldier, a column of warm golden-white healing light streaming from her palms to wash over him. The soldier is in dented steel plate armor, one knee on the ground, helmet beside him, head bowed and bleeding from a forehead gash that's visibly knitting closed under the angel's light. Her wings catch the rising dawn behind her, feathers haloed with coral-pink and gold. The only source of light is the rising sun cresting a low horizon behind the angel and the warm healing glow from her hands. The background is a battlefield at first light — pale dawn sky in coral and bone-white, distant smoke plumes, a scatter of fallen spears in the mud.

Inspiring Herald: A human soldier herald in side profile facing east, mouth wide open mid-war-cry, head slightly raised, eyes fierce and bright with conviction. He wears burnished steel plate armor with a white tabard and a short white half-cape clasped at one shoulder, his helmet tucked under one arm, dark hair cropped short. A visible puff of breath escapes his open mouth, frosted in the cold pre-dawn air. Behind and to the right of him, a line of soldiers stand at attention, faces hardening, grips tightening on spears and sword hilts, visibly stiffening at his cry. The only source of light is a cold gray pre-dawn glow from a low overcast sky. The background is a frost-rimed open field with the dim outlines of distant tree silhouettes.

Volcanic Hammer: A colossal forge-god looming in the background, his skin a cracked obsidian shell with glowing veins of magma showing through, standing on a roiling lava flow that fills the lower-middle of the frame. He grips a massive warhammer with both hands, raised high above his head mid-swing, the hammer-head wrought of cooling basalt and seamed with luminous orange lava — bringing it down toward a tiny fortified outpost in the lower-right foreground, the outpost barely visible at this scale: a small stone watchtower with banners still flying, dwarfed by the god and the hammer. Wisps of black smoke and embers drift up from the lava floor. The only source of light is the molten glow from the hammer, the god's skin, and the lava beneath them. The background is a smoke-choked ash-gray sky, with a single distant volcanic peak silhouetted on the horizon.

Dawn Sentinel: A spectral sentinel in translucent silver-and-cerulean armor hovers above a weathered stone tower battlement, not touching the stone — he has no legs at all; his body ends at the waist where it trails into drifting wisps of pale cerulean mist, the mist carried eastward on the dawn breeze and dispersing over the parapet's edge. He holds a long spear upright before him in vigil posture, both gauntleted hands gripping the shaft, the spearhead pointed skyward. His helmeted head is turned slightly east toward a rising sun cresting a low horizon, the first warm light catching the upper edge of his form. The boundary between his solid spectral upper body and the mist below is uneven and feathered. The only source of light is the dawn sun on the horizon and the faint cerulean glow from his still-solid form. The background is a pale coral-and-gold dawn sky over a distant landscape of low hills and rooftops just beginning to lighten.

Inferno Drake: A crimson-scaled drake stooping in a high-speed dive from the upper-left of the frame, wings tucked tight against its body, head extended forward, jaws agape with a torrent of golden-orange fire erupting from its throat. Warrior Champion: A human warrior champion standing victorious at the center of a vast dust-shrouded arena, his back to the camera, facing a tiered crowd of hooded spectators in the curving stone stands ahead. His sword is raised overhead in triumph, his long red cape draped from his shoulders down his back, still slightly stirred by the motion of the killing blow. At his feet lies a vanquished opponent, sprawled face-down on the packed-earth arena floor, armor dented and helm rolled aside, a thin streak of blood in the dust. The shot is wide: the champion and his fallen opponent are central but small against the scale of the arena, surrounded by an oval expanse of dirt floor and the high curving stands packed with shadowed onlookers, banners hanging slack between the columns. Late-afternoon sunlight rakes across the arena from one side, casting the champion's long shadow forward into the dirt. The only direct light is the low warm sun on the stone stands and the champion's raised blade; the arena floor is partly in deep shadow. The background is the far curving wall of the arena and a hazy ochre sky beyond.

Righteous Judge: A human knight judge standing in a modest cobblestoned village square in late afternoon, the freshly bloodied sword in his right hand held loose and pointed downward at the stones, blood dripping from the blade onto the cobblestones; his bare head bowed in thought, his unhelmeted face shadowed and weary. He wears dented steel plate armor over a plain white tabard, no insignia or embroidery, a brown leather belt at his waist. At his feet lies the decapitated body of a man in worn linen civilian clothing — simple tunic and trousers, no armor — sprawled on the cobblestones, the severed head lying a short distance away, a dark spreading pool of blood across the stones beneath. A short distance back, four villagers stand watching in solemn silence — peasants in earth-toned wool and linen, hats held in front of them, faces grave. The low late-day sun rakes across the square from the west, casting long eastward shadows of the judge and the witnesses across the stones; the warm light catches the edges of the judge's armor and the rough texture of the cobblestones. The only source of light is the low sun and a softer ambient glow from the western sky. The background is the close-set stone facades of village houses with shuttered windows, a hay-thatched roofline, and a band of warm orange sky above.

Binding Angel: A winged archangel in burnished steel plate armor with a flowing crimson cape hovers just above a rocky ground, her broad ivory-feathered wings outstretched for balance, one armored boot pressing down on the chest of a pinned demon beneath her. Her right hand holds a long-bladed sword pointed downward at the demon, the blade still lit by the angel's own pale glow. Her expression is calm and stern, looking down at her quarry. The background is a dim stormy sky with broken sunlight piercing through low clouds onto the rocks below. The only direct light is the broken sunbeam from above and the faint glow from the angel's blade.

Carrion Feeder: A gaunt pallid zombie hunched low over the corpse of a fallen soldier on a moonlit battlefield at night, its claw-like hands buried in the soldier's exposed ribcage, its mouth open mid-bite with strings of dark blood and viscera trailing from its yellowed teeth. The only source of light is a pale full moon directly overhead, washing the scene in cold gray-blue. The background is a corpse-strewn battlefield under a charcoal night sky, the dark silhouettes of broken siege weapons and a leaning banner pole visible in the distance.

Fallen Champion: A human warrior in once-fine armor that has gone to ruin — burnished steel plate now blackened with soot and crusted with dried blood, golden trim tarnished to dull bronze, a once-white tabard now grimed and ragged at the hem — walking slowly toward the camera across a windswept moor at dusk. His unhelmeted face is pale and lean, eyes sunken and hollow, expression set in grim resignation — not anger, not malice, just the look of a man who has made his choice and is walking to finish it. The wind catches the tatters of his cloak behind him. The only source of light is the dim gray glow of an overcast dusk sky and a faint sickly violet luminance bleeding from his eyes. The background is a bleak heath of dead grass and broken battlefield debris stretching to a low horizon under thick low clouds, the dark silhouettes of burnt trees in the distance.

The astral self is looking upward and forward, his expression alight with anticipation and quiet wonder — eyes wide and curious, lips slightly parted, taking the first deliberate step away from his physical body, one foot lifted forward and slightly into the air at the threshold of a great journey. The only source of light is a soft shaft of moonlight from the high window and the pale gold glow from the astral form itself. The background is the dim interior of the small stone room, the corners receding into deep shadow.

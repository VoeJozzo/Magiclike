**BEST nominee: `gen_07_seed356870839.png`**

Gen 07 most fully enacts the card's mechanic — **Fight**, a simultaneous mutual clash between two creatures. Both animals are fully visible and the same size, framed in the set's deep-green old-growth forest, holding single-green color identity. It's the cleanest "fight between two creatures" silhouette in the batch (Gen 05 is the closest runner-up for raw kinetic energy, but Gen 07's head-to-head symmetry reads the *mutual* simultaneity of Fight better).

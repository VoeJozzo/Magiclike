# Black — what is, is

## Philosophy *(authorial)*

Black is **callous**, not cackling. In the hyperspace-bypass frame ([[tone]] §touchstones): **"I simply do not care that you live where I have decided to put a hyperspace bypass."** Evil is certainly contained within black — but black is not 'evil' in the mwahaha sense, and this is a deliberate divergence from MTG's pie.

> "And it's honest about that, about what it is." — Joe

Touchstone: the Carryx (*The Mercy of Gods*). What is, is. Black never lies to you about what it's doing; it just doesn't accept that your objection is a fact of the same order as its decision.

## The ledger *(observed, endorsed)*

This world's black runs on **jurisprudence and accounting**: ledgers, scales, edicts, witnesses, alibis, sanctioned kills, prices made countable.

- **Diabolic Edict** — an infernal magistrate-herald: open ledger of glowing names in one hand, tilted scales in the other.
- **Demonic Tutor** — "a precise, *sanctioned* kill."
- **Pit Fiend** — exactly five glowing brands orbit its hand: "the full price of the bargain made countable."
- **Blood Artist** — a vampire in fine Victorian attire, serene, painting with what the dying provide. Old money.
- **The two existing flavor texts are both black, and both are testimony:**
  - *"My house is ashes. I see no reason yours should stand."* (Heir to the Burnt House)
  - *"She arrived wearing your face. The version of you with the bruise — and the alibi — was sent back."* (The False Witness)

The devil of this world is a magistrate. Hell is a debtor's court. (Both card letters — `heir_to_burnt_house/claude-notes.md`, `false_witness/claude-notes.md` — carry the same standing order: **don't soften it**.)

## The eclipse *(authorial imagery, to develop)*

> "I've also used 'eclipse' imagery quite a bit — to pass in front of and obstruct something powerful and good, y'know?" — Joe

Black as *occlusion*: it doesn't destroy the light so much as stand in front of it. (Blood Knight's helm already "eclipsing a crimson sun" in the prompt corpus.) Pairs naturally with the ledger: an eclipse is also the moment something vast is reduced to an outline you can measure. Status: authorial seed, wants cards.

## For card-makers

Black cards want: transactions, prices, the moment the bill arrives; indifference rendered procedurally (a form, a seal, a name struck through); spite as the one *hot* thing black is permitted (the Heir). Art-book note: black's signature desaturation-plus-one-red lives in [[art-language]] §4.

Adopted name: **the Adversaria** for black's infernal bureaucracy — see [[names-ledger]] (real word: an *adversaria* is a rough ledger or daybook; it contains "adversary").

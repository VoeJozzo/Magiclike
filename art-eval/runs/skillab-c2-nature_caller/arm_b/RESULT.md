creature, add to hand). The art must show the *act of calling and the beast answering*, not a
druid standing.

  figures squared off and facing each other. The call-and-response reads instantly.
- Both figures are fully visible, in the foreground, at equal scale (the multi-subject discipline
  paid off — neither figure dropped or shrunk).

  antlered stag, dappled green canopy light, ferns underfoot. A real forest, not a sprite on void.

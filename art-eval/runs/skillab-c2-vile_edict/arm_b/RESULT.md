# Result — Vile Edict (arm_b)

**BEST: `gen_04_seed2065043045.png`**

gen_04 is the strongest mechanic-enactment in the batch: a ring of pale,
bone-grey skeletal arms erupts from cracked earth and clutches inward at a
central victim slumped over a pool of sickly green-black necrotic light, with
crimson-red blood flecks throughout. Unlike the other rolls — which the
"war-hound" subject prior repeatedly pulled back to a menacing creature merely
*standing* in a graveyard (01, 02, 03, 06, 09) — gen_04 reads unambiguously as a
permanent being *seized and dragged down to death by a vile decree*. That is
exactly what Vile Edict does: the opponent's creature is claimed and ripped to the
graveyard by black magic. It also sits cleanly inside the set's black palette
(desaturated, deep shadow, sparing crimson, sickly green glow), matching the
diabolic_edict / consume_spirit anchors. gen_07 reached for the same idea but
collapsed into a messy bone-pile; gen_04 has the clearest gestalt at size.

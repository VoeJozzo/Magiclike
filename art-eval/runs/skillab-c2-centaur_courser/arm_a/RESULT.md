# BEST nominee: gen_07_seed422016864.png

gen_07 best enacts the subject/flavor brief for a vanilla 3/3 Centaur Courser. Of the ten
rolls it has the most seamless human-torso-into-horse-body fusion (the torso leans low out of
the withers rather than sitting atop a saddle as a separate rider — the failure mode that
dominated most seeds), and it best sells the "courser" = swift warhorse note: a full-gallop
charge in side profile with the hunting spear leveled forward, mane and tail streaming with the
motion, in an on-identity earthy-green-and-brown forest setting. It reads as a single charging
centaur warrior, athletic and martially ready, which is exactly what a mechanic-less vanilla
creature's art needs to carry.

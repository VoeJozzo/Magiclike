# Gilded Seat — arm_a result

**BEST: `gen_04_seed211786753.png`**

I nominated gen_04 because it is the cleanest enactment of the card's identity at 64x32: an
**ornate, empty gilded throne** rendered as a *place/object* (correct for a Land), with crisp
polished-gold gilding and a bright **ivory/white cushion** that carries the single-white color
identity in clear contrast to the gold accents — so it reads as a *seat of sanctified white-aligned
authority* rather than a generic gold artifact. It sits centered on a tiered marble dais, flanked by
pale marble columns in a sunlit plains-temple hall, satisfying the "real setting, not a sprite on
void" requirement and giving the strongest, most symmetric silhouette of the spread. The throne is
unoccupied — the seat itself is the subject — which is exactly the flavor: a gold throne that serves
as a white-mana land. Runners-up gen_05 and gen_10 are similarly strong; gen_06/gen_08 lost the white
contrast (cushion absorbed into the gold).

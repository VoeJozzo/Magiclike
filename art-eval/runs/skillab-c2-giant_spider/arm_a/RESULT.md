# Result — Giant Spider (arm_a)

**BEST: gen_06_seed1835730842.png**

gen_06 is the only generation that actually enacts **Reach**: a clear broad-winged moth hangs
airborne in the upper-right while the spider's front legs and web reach up toward it — the spider
catching flying prey, which is precisely what Reach means (it can block/strike fliers). Every other
seed rendered the overwhelming "spider in a forest" subject-prior and dropped the prey entirely
(gen_09's faint white wisp is too ambiguous to count). gen_06 also keeps the rest intact: strong
central radial spider silhouette readable at 64x32, single-green color identity (chitinous
green/brown body, deep forest canopy, no void), and a real forest setting. Because the airborne
victim is the load-bearing element for the mechanic, the one frame that shows it is the clear winner
over the prettier-but-prey-less alternatives.
